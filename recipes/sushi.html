Homemade Sushi

Homemade Sushi is so much cheaper than at the restaurant. Plus, it’s easy and fun to make your own rolls at home, so you can put all your favorite ingredients into your perfect custom roll — here’s how!

Ingredients:

  • 6 sheets sushi seaweed aka nori
  • 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  • 1/2 lb sashimi-grade raw salmon or desired raw fish of choice
  • 4 oz cream cheese sliced into strips
  • 1 avocado sliced
  • soy sauce for serving

Steps:

  1. Brown your hamburger in a large pan.
  2. Once cooked, throw in salt, pepper, tomato sauce and paste, water (with the bouillon cubes in it), sugar, basil, oregano and garlic. Simmer on low for an hour.
  3. A few minutes before the hour is done, cook box of spaghetti noodles as directed on package.
  4. Once the noodles are cooked, drain and add to spaghetti sauce. ENJOY!
